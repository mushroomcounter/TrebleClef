This cache is a direct replacement for TC#26 Chemical Devotion.  It has been placed at the same location, just a little more securely.  Please replace the cache hidden as found and let me know if any maintenance is needed. Thank you.

Welcome to cache number 26 in the Treble Clef Series. The series has been created using a theme that most people can find enjoyment in, in some form or another - music. It includes a wide range of styles, but I apologise if your personal favourite is not included.

These caches can be solved by older kids and teenagers, but will be a bit of a challenge to the younger ones I'm afraid, and the puzzles do become more challenging towards the end of the series. But as usual, all of the cache containers will be as big as possible and most will include at least a few swappables, so the actual walks will be enjoyable for all the family.

The caches are set out in 3 separate circular walks of between 2- 4 miles each. Please forgive any "gaps" in the circuits; these exist solely to avoid pre-existing caches, which you can also pick up on your way round, if you haven't done so already.

This is no physical cache at this point. To find the cache, you need to solve the puzzle below to obtain the cache co-ordinates.

The cache is a small container with just enough room for a logbook. There is no room for swag or trackables. There is no pen, so please bring your own.

Thanks to Sallilas-Harris Clan who created the series and placed the original version of this cache and to Nognod who created the geoart.

All the caches have been placed with kind permission from the National Trust.

Enjoy!
